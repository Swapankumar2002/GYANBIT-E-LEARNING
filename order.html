📋 Guidelines & Instructions

Welcome to the Website Order Portal of Swapan Kumar Mondal. We're delighted to help bring your online presence to life. Whether you're looking for a personal portfolio, a business website, or a full-fledged e-commerce platform, we aim to make the process smooth, transparent, and rewarding. Please take a moment to carefully read the following guidelines and instructions before submitting your website order request.

Before placing an order, it's essential to have a clear understanding of what kind of website you need. Think about the purpose of your website: Is it to showcase your personal projects and skills? Promote your business or services? Sell products online? Share blog posts or educational resources? Knowing your goal will help us build a website that’s aligned with your vision.

We offer various types of websites depending on your needs, including Portfolio, Business, E-commerce, Blog, Educational, and Custom solutions. Be specific about your requirements when filling out the form.

If you already have your content like text files, images, logos, or design references, upload them to Google Drive and paste the link into the form. This helps us align the site with your branding.

Once we receive your order, we’ll review the details and contact you within 24–48 hours for confirmation and clarification. After confirmation, our development process includes design, development, feedback/revision stages, testing, and final delivery. You’ll be kept updated throughout the process.

We provide support for 30 days after delivery, covering minor fixes, bug resolution, and basic hosting help. Extended maintenance is also available.

Basic websites like portfolios are free, while advanced features like logins, payments, and dashboards may incur a small fee. You’ll be informed upfront before we begin.

Average delivery times vary:

  • Portfolio – 2–3 days
  • Business – 4–6 days
  • E-commerce – 7–10 days
  • Custom – Based on scope
We also value your privacy. Your data will not be shared with anyone, and all files remain confidential. You can also request an NDA if needed.
